University Student

You're a regular college student, always scrolling—between classes, at night, even during meals. Lately, your feed feels off. It’s louder, more extreme. Posts seem designed to rile you up or suck you in. You start to question: should you keep trusting what the app shows you, or take the time to look elsewhere and hear different sides?

Trust the Feed: Stick with what the app gives you. It’s fast, familiar, and always keeps you scrolling. Look Around: Step outside the algorithm. Search for different views, even if it takes more effort.
